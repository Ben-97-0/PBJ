PB&J Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly ( Grape Jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut Butter on one slice
  3. Spread Jelly on the other side
  4. Put the 2 slices together
